What We Do

Beacon leverage's a unique network of industry operators to collect the best operational
data available from a wide variety of participants. We collect microeconomic data from any and all operators,
permitted and non-permitted alike, inside and outside California

We have developed a set of tools and market indicators that directly measure REAL industry metrics that
drive operator decision-making. We help both regulators and operators understand operational costs, realistic
efficiencies and yields, retail and wholesale pricing, and demand forces

Our mission is to understand what makes the California Cannabis Industry tick
